# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism.
- Focus on what is best for the community.
- Show empathy toward other community members.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Violence, threats, or discriminatory jokes and language.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without permission.
- Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Responsibilities and Enforcement
Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards. They may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct, or ban contributors for abusive behavior.

## Scope
This Code applies within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual represents the project or its community.

## Reporting
Report issues to the maintainers via GitHub issues or direct contact. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
